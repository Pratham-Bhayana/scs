Risk Management and Analytics & Platform

SCS Response Platform calculates the impact of security threats, and the likelihood of risks happening, from a single location.

Create, Manage, Track, and Mitigate Risks

Startups Cyber Security makes the creation of risks easy with a simple 3-click process, and a library of threat profiles available to readily use.
Document, track, and mitigate your own risks. Within the risk itself, use individual mitigation trackers. Easily create risks, identify suspected risks, and assign risks to specific actions. Gain access to a library of threat profiles, with relevant and generic mitigations and threat mapping.

Measure Security Risk Against Exposure Score

Know your exposure score to understand how vulnerable your business is to cyber threats.
Easily define the priority and severity of the risk using the exposure risk score, which is calculated based on combining the attribute source, impact, likelihood, and weakness.

Use Mitigation Control Tracker to Reduce Overall Business Risk

For a single risk, individually assign different action owners for different teams. Ensure currency of knowledge in a changing technical environment. Safeguard against hefty fines and maintain brand value.
