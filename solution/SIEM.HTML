SIEM Detection & Response

Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) Detection & Response offers round-the-clock, continuous monitoring and in-depth analysis of security events and logs through a centralized, intelligent platform. By aggregating and correlating data from various sources, SIEM enhances threat detection, incident response, and compliance management.

Key Benefits

Reduced Noise

62% lower noise-to-signal ratio than other competitors.

Risk Reduction

Reduce attack surface and maximize the ROI of your existing cyber security technologies.

Complete Visibility

SCS Response Platform allows you to visualize, prioritize, escalate and respond to incidents.

Expert Team

450+ security experts, threat hunters, and SANS GIAC certified Incident handlers and forensic analysts.

What Does SIEM Detection & Response Do?

Our SIEM Detection & Response alerts you to what is important, provides actions on elements that need investigation, automates activities, and rapidly responds to threats. SIEM also helps organizations stay compliant and up to date with security audits, by enhancing overall security posture.

Tech Agnostic SIEM Analytics

Ingestion and orchestration of data from a wide-range of sources including logs, events, and alerts using all major SIEM vendors. Use Our SIEM or Bring Your Own SIEM for us to manage.

24/7 Detection

SCS Cyber Defense Team triage and investigates threats from correlated alerts, 24/7. Threat detection rules are synchronized to SIEM platforms and events are triaged in seconds.

24/7 Response

SCS Incident Response team contains threats to manage the impact of an Incident and provide continuous support for handling threats.
